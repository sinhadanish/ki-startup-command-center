## Revenue Model Overview

### Freemium SaaS Strategy
Ki operates a freemium model designed to demonstrate value before conversion:

#### Free Tier
- **2 Free Conversations**: Allow couples to experience Ki's relationship intelligence
- **Basic Pattern Recognition**: Limited insights to showcase capability
- **Crisis Support**: Essential safety features remain accessible
- **Conversion Focus**: Designed to drive subscription adoption

#### Premium Subscription
- **Launch Pricing**: $19.99/month (both partners included)
- **Mature Pricing**: $29.99/month (18-month target)
- **Unlimited Access**: 24/7 relationship support and crisis intervention
- **Advanced Features**: Deep pattern recognition, relationship memory, personalized insights
- **Professional Integration**: Therapist connections and referral support

### Strategic Partnerships (20% of Growth)
**Strategy**: Corporate wellness and health platform integration
- **CAC**: $25 per customer
- **Partners**: Health insurers, employee wellness programs
- **B2B Pricing**: $5-10 per employee per month
- **ROI**: 18x lifetime value

### Viral Referrals (5% of Growth)
**Strategy**: Couples sharing relationship breakthroughs
- **CAC**: $15 per customer (incentive costs)

- **Organic Growth**: Relationship success drives natural sharing
- **ROI**: 30x lifetime value
