## Investor Presentation Best Practices

### Presentation Flow
1. **Start Strong**: Open with compelling metrics and tagline
2. **Build Context**: Clearly articulate the problem and solution
3. **Show Traction**: Demonstrate early validation and growth
4. **Explain Opportunity**: Market size and competitive advantage
5. **Present Team**: Credibility and execution capability
6. **Close with Ask**: Clear funding request and use of funds

### Key Messaging
- **Differentiation**: Human-AI-Human framework is unique and defensible
- **Market Timing**: Convergence of AI capability and relationship support demand
- **Traction**: Strong early metrics with zero marketing investment
- **Vision**: Building the "Relationship OS" for the future
- **Team**: Experienced founders with proven execution track record
